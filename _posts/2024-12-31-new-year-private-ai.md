The [open source AI community](/blog/2024/09/19/open-source-ai-power-of-community/) played a central role in making these breakthroughs possible. Perhaps most importantly, 2024 marked a shift in public consciousness about AI privacy. As more users experienced the benefits of offline, private AI, the demand for privacy-respecting technology has grown exponentially. This growing awareness has accelerated development in the field, leading to better optimization techniques and more sophisticated local processing capabilities.
