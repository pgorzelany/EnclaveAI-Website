Common questions

How exactly do credits work?

Your $9.99/month subscription gives you $9.99 in cloud credits — every cent of your subscription goes toward AI usage, with no markup. Credits reset at the start of each billing period. Unused credits don't carry over to the next month.

How do I cancel?

Your subscription is managed by Apple. You can cancel anytime through the App Store or your device's subscription settings. You'll keep Pro access until the end of the current billing period.

Is there a free trial?

The app itself is free — there's no time limit on using local AI. The Pro subscription is only needed when you want to use cloud models. Try the free tier first and upgrade when you're ready.

What happens when my credits run out?

Local AI keeps working as normal — it's always free and unlimited. Cloud models become unavailable until your credits reset at the start of your next billing period.

Can I use cloud models without subscribing?

Not currently through Enclave's built-in system. Cloud access requires a Pro subscription. Local models — including many capable open-source ones — are always free and need no internet connection.
